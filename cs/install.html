After install: drop in your first project

Installed isn't done. First project being protected the same day is done.

  1. 1

    Open Keeply, click "New project"

    Pick a folder you're actively working in — a pitch, a contract, a design file. Don't pick an archive folder you haven't touched in six months.

  2. 2

    Wait 1–2 minutes for the first scan

    Keeply indexes the folder once. After that it watches in the background and records versions automatically as you save.

  3. 3

    Keep working as normal

    No manual "save snapshot" button. Just hit save in your editor like you always do. Three days later when you need to roll back, history is there.
